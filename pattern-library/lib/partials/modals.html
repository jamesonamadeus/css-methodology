Inline Modals

Inline modals do not obscure the entire screen and can be interacted with, or ignored, by the end user.

Inline modals can either appear in the normal document flow, but their typical use-case involves them being absolutely positioned near the trigger of the inline modal.

Inline styles have been added to these modals for UI Kit display only
